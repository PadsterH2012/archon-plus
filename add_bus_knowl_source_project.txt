# Business Knowledge Source - Project Management

## Project: Archon Plus Business Requirements

### Project Objectives
The Archon Plus project aims to create an intelligent task management and knowledge base system that reduces cognitive load for developers and improves project delivery efficiency.

### Business Goals

#### Primary Objectives
1. **Reduce Context Switching**: Minimize time spent remembering project details
2. **Improve Documentation**: Automatic capture and retrieval of project knowledge
3. **Enhance Productivity**: Streamline development workflows
4. **Knowledge Retention**: Prevent loss of institutional knowledge

#### Success Metrics
- 50% reduction in time spent searching for project information
- 75% improvement in onboarding new team members
- 90% of project decisions documented automatically
- Zero critical knowledge loss during team transitions

### Stakeholder Requirements

#### Development Team Needs
- Quick access to credentials and configuration details
- Automatic task tracking and progress monitoring
- Context-aware assistance for complex projects
- Seamless integration with existing tools

#### Management Requirements
- Project visibility and progress tracking
- Resource allocation optimization
- Risk identification and mitigation
- Compliance and audit trail maintenance

### Business Processes

#### Project Lifecycle
1. **Initiation**: Create project with PRP (Product Requirement Prompt)
2. **Planning**: Break down into manageable tasks
3. **Execution**: Track progress and maintain context
4. **Monitoring**: Continuous feedback and adjustment
5. **Closure**: Document lessons learned and outcomes

#### Knowledge Management Workflow
1. **Capture**: Automatic documentation of decisions and changes
2. **Organize**: Structured storage with searchable metadata
3. **Retrieve**: Intelligent search and context-aware suggestions
4. **Update**: Continuous refinement and improvement
5. **Archive**: Long-term preservation of critical knowledge

### Resource Allocation

#### Team Structure
- **Project Lead**: Overall coordination and decision making
- **Technical Lead**: Architecture and implementation oversight
- **Developers**: Feature implementation and testing
- **QA Engineer**: Quality assurance and validation
- **DevOps Engineer**: Infrastructure and deployment

#### Budget Considerations
- Infrastructure costs (servers, storage, networking)
- Software licensing (development tools, monitoring)
- Training and development (team skill enhancement)
- Maintenance and support (ongoing operational costs)

### Risk Management

#### Identified Risks
1. **Technical Complexity**: Advanced AI integration challenges
2. **User Adoption**: Resistance to new workflows
3. **Data Security**: Sensitive information protection
4. **Performance**: System scalability under load
5. **Integration**: Compatibility with existing systems

#### Mitigation Strategies
- Incremental development with regular feedback
- Comprehensive training and support programs
- Robust security framework implementation
- Performance testing and optimization
- Careful integration planning and testing

### Compliance Requirements

#### Data Protection
- GDPR compliance for EU data
- Secure storage and transmission
- Access control and audit logging
- Data retention and deletion policies

#### Quality Standards
- ISO 9001 quality management
- Agile development methodologies
- Continuous integration and deployment
- Regular security assessments
